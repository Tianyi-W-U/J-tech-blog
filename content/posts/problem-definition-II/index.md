## Design Probes
Better uncovering people's action than their opnion

Types can be:
- Logging material
- Cameras
- Prototypes
e.g.,
1. to-go probe kits
2. low-fidelity prototypes
    - testing the problem space (how people will use it, more UX?), not a solution
    - e.g., test the size of product, e.g., a phone
3. Role play when you are designing a service
    - Will find, say, if the size is too small or too big, will the user use it intuitively

### What to look for?
Anything weird or intersting!
- Contradictions: Says A, does B
- Workarounds: How do users deal with the prototype which still misses things?
- More...[slides]
Learn **context**!

## Refine the problem statement with probes
1. Define a direction
2. Create a small probe to explore this direction
3. Try it youself tor pass it to stakeholders and observe
